EVO+ ICL, 안내렌즈삽입술

EVO+ ICL is an lens that corrects nearsightedness, with and without astigmatism, through a minimally invasive procedure.

EVO ICL lens is implanted between your iris (the colored part of the eye) and your natural crystalline lens in a quick and safe 20–30 minute procedure. It’s biocompatible and does not remove corneal tissue, so it works with your natural eye and the lens implant is reversible.
